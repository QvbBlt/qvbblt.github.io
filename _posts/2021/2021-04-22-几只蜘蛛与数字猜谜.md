![ ](https://i.loli.net/2021/04/22/PXEfKNMJbwHCUl9.png)

```latex
\documentclass{article}
\begin{document}
8(8-x)+6x=54\par
8*8-54=(8-6)x
\end{document}
```

$$
\begin{matrix}
        8(8-x)+6x=54\\
        8*8-54=(8-6)x
\end{matrix}
$$

![ ](https://i.loli.net/2021/04/22/1DsSAGxeyJP9a2Q.png)

```latex
\documentclass{article}
\begin{document}
((x+1)3+1-4) \div 4
\end{document}
```

$$
\begin{matrix}
        ((x+1)3+1-4) \div 4\\
        3x+3-1-4
\end{matrix}
$$
